What is Catgarten ?

You want to go on holiday or have to go away but no one to look after your cat? Catgarten is there for you, you drop your cat(s) off and pick them up on your return

In a big house (4000 sq feet) with garden, your cat(s) will have all the space they need, and we'll make sure nothing bad happens to them.
